La Phat Thoke

La Phat Thoke is a traditional Burmese fermented tea leaf salad,
especially popular in Myanmar's Shan State. It combines tangy, savory tea leaves
with crunchy nuts, fresh vegetables, and a bold dressing for a unique, vibrant dish.

Ingredients

  • Fermented tea leaves
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Tomatoes
  • Roasted peanuts
  • Sesame seeds
  • Garlic oil
  • Fish sauce and lime juice
  • Dried shrimp or fried garlic

The Process: The Art of Making Laphatthoke

  1. Lightly crush the fermented tea leaves.
  2. Mix tea leaves with tomatoes, cabbage, and crispy garlic.
  3. Add peanuts, sesame seeds, and dried shrimp for texture.
  4. Toss with fish sauce, lime juice, and a touch of chili.
  5. Serve the salad fresh with crunchy toppings on top.
